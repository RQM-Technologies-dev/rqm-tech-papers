This repository is the publishing home for RQM Technologies technical papers. Each paper package includes a human-readable HTML and PDF surface plus machine-readable metadata, claims, notation, glossary, references, and JATS XML for downstream indexing.

Foundations of Quaternionic Quantum Computing: Qubits, Spinors, and SU(2) Geometry

John G. Van Geem

Conservative foundation paper on normalized spinors, the Bloch sphere, SU(2), and the unit-quaternion model of single-qubit gates.

What a paper package includes

Each paper directory is organized for both publication and machine consumption.

For human readers

  • index.html as the clean package route and paper.html as the full paper page
  • paper.pdf as the printable paper artifact
  • figures and supporting artifacts inside artifacts/

For AI and indexing

  • metadata.json for structured discovery
  • claims.json, notation.json, and glossary.json
  • paper.jats.xml and CITATION.cff for exchange formats

For authors

  • README.md declaring the source of truth
  • main.tex for LaTeX and Overleaf workflows when applicable
  • starter scaffolding available under templates/
