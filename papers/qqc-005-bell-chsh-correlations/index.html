Paper package

qqc-005-bell-chsh-correlations

Package landing page for Bell and CHSH Correlations from Quaternionic Spin Geometry: A Direct Derivation on S^3. This paper derives the singlet Bell correlation law and the CHSH bound in the fixed quaternion-SU(2) convention of the RQM series, and includes a small reference script that reproduces the listed values.

Package overview

This paper depends on Foundations of Quaternionic Quantum Computing: Qubits, Spinors, and SU(2) Geometry for the fixed quaternion-SU(2) convention and extends the series into Bell/CHSH correlation geometry.
