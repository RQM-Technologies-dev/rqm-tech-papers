qqc-003-gate-fusion

Package landing page for Quaternionic Gate Fusion on S3: Geometry-Native Optimization for Single-Qubit Circuit Segments. This paper studies a local single-qubit segment fusion pass built on the canonical quaternionic IR introduced in A Canonical Quaternionic Intermediate Representation for Single-Qubit Quantum Circuits.

Read the HTML paper Open the PDF

Package overview

The paper depends on Foundations of Quaternionic Quantum Computing: Qubits, Spinors, and SU(2) Geometry and A Canonical Quaternionic Intermediate Representation for Single-Qubit Quantum Circuits, and adds a measured reference corpus for local quaternionic gate fusion on single-qubit segments.
