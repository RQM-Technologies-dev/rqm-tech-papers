Package landing page for Quaternionic Gate Fusion on S3: Geometry-Native Optimization for Single-Qubit Circuit Segments. This paper studies a local single-qubit segment fusion pass built on the canonical quaternionic IR introduced in paper 2.

Package overview

The paper depends on qqc-001-foundations and qqc-002-canonical-ir, and adds a measured reference corpus for local quaternionic gate fusion on single-qubit segments.
